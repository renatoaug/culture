Organizational structure_

Functional roles_

In addition to formal roles, we have functional roles that can be assumed temporarily according to the strategic needs of the squad or tribe. These roles do not generate promotions or accumulation of functions, but represent opportunities for personal and professional development, contributing directly to the growth of our teams and the success of our projects.

RACI table_

We use the RACI model to clearly define the responsibilities of each role in relation to tasks and decisions. The RACI is a management tool that helps ensure that everyone knows who is responsible, who has authority, who should be consulted and who needs to be informed about each activity. This promotes transparency and efficiency, minimizing conflicts and ensuring that all collaborators are aligned with their duties and expectations.

Responsible - who executes?
Authority - who has the final decision?
Consulted - who opines?
Informed - who is informed?
